# `fusion/` — Three Options Distilled From the 8 Shortlist

This folder is the answer to: *"of the 8 ideas I shortlisted, which can we actually build, sell, and impress 2026 judges with — possibly by merging?"*

**Start here:** [`FUSED.md`](./FUSED.md) — the final menu. 3 maximally-distinct options. Pick one.

## How this was produced

A 4-teammate agent team (`idea-fusion`) analyzed every shortlisted idea + 4 candidate fusions in parallel through three lenses, then a fusion architect synthesized the final menu. The three lenses **disagreed sharply** — that disagreement is preserved in the final document instead of averaged out.
