### Highest pitch-impact for a generalist judge

**#04 SLA Insurance — "Slash Bezos"** — the headline is too sticky to ignore. Every dev has been bitten by an AWS outage. The pitch lands in 11 words and *each judge personally feels it*. Pairs with EigenLayer narrative.

**#02 Parametric Cat Pool** — $63.9B outstanding cat-bond market (Artemis Q1 2026 record), 30-second settlement vs Bermuda SPV's 90 days. Visceral, universally relatable, paper-backed (Wang multi-event cat bond model), live oracle feeds already exist.

**#09 SensorBond** — DePIN × insurance hybrid. *"Your rain gauge IS your insurance policy"* collapses three industries (sensor, broker, payout) into one piece of hardware. Strong community-serving angle (smallholder farmers) directly maps onto the GuardChain Bengaluru-winner pattern.

### Highest creative-uplift / fresh-narrative

**#06 Music Royalty Variance Swap** — Hipgnosis blew up tabloid-style ($2.2B Blackstone acquisition). Trading the *vol* of catalogs is a new asset class with zero TradFi precedent. Carr-Madan-replication math = Stylus-canonical.

**#07 Baltic Pool — Freight Derivatives** — most people don't know the $30B+ FFA market exists. The Ever-Given Suez 2021 mental anchor is universal. Pairs with Hyperliquid HIP-3 narrative as the proof that *exotic* permissionless perps work.
